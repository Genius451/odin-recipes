Dubu Jorim

Description

Dubu jorim is sliced and seared tofu that has been simmered in a sweet and slightly spicy soy-based sauce. Lightly crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, itâ€™s an easy-to-prepare side dish for breakfast, dinner, or a traditional Korean dosirak (packed lunch).

Ingredients

For the seared tofu

  • 1 (15 to 18-ounce) package firm tofu
  • 3 tablespoons rice flour
  • 2 tablespoons canola or vegetable oil, or as needed

For the sauce

  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup water, plus more as needed
  • 1 tablespoon gochugaru
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced, divided
